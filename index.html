Our Trip to KIKK Festival 2024

WHAT WE DID ?

During our two days at the KIKK Festival in Namur, we explored interactive installations, attended inspiring talks from artists and innovators, and participated in workshops focused on digital art and creative technology.

About the Festival

The KIKK Festival is a hub of creativity and innovation, bringing together artists, designers, and technologists from around the globe. It provides a platform to explore the intersections of art, science, and technology through engaging exhibitions, thought-provoking talks, and hands-on workshops. Our experience at the festival left us inspired and eager to dive deeper into the world of digital creativity.
